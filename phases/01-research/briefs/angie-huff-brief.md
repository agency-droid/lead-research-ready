## What She Does (The Offer)

SYNC Life is an integrative medicine practice where a team of a PharmD, IFM-certified MD, and nurse practitioner work together to identify the root causes of why you feel "off" — not just manage symptoms. They measure 124 wellness biomarkers (including 54 cellular micronutrient levels), then build a fully personalized wellness plan spanning hormone optimization, IV therapy, peptides, NAD+ infusions, weight management, aesthetics, and professional-grade supplements. Built on 18+ years of Angie watching patients leave with more prescriptions and less health — and deciding to build something different. Their process: **Assess → Plan → Optimize.**
