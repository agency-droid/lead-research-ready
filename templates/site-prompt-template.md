## LOVABLE BUILD PROMPT

```
Build a professional, high-converting landing page for [PROSPECT NAME], a [TITLE / ROLE] who helps [TARGET AUDIENCE] with [WHAT THEY DO].

---

ABOUT THIS PERSON:
[PASTE 2-3 SENTENCES FROM RESEARCH BRIEF — what they do, who they help, what makes them stand out]

---

PAGE STRUCTURE:
Build these sections in order:

1. HERO SECTION
- Bold headline: [WRITE OR SUGGEST A HEADLINE BASED ON THEIR OFFER — e.g., "Baton Rouge's Go-To Marketing Strategist for Local Businesses"]
- Subheadline: [1 line — what they help clients achieve — e.g., "Helping service businesses grow through strategy, content, and execution"]
- CTA button: "Book a Free Strategy Call" (prominently visible, above the fold)
- Background: [DARK/LIGHT based on their brand tone]

2. ABOUT / VALUE SECTION
- Short paragraph about who they are and why they're the right choice
- [USE THEIR DIFFERENTIATOR HERE]
- Optional: headshot placeholder if they have one

3. WHAT I DO / SERVICES SECTION
- List their 3-4 main services or areas of expertise
- Keep descriptions short (1-2 sentences each)
- Services: [LIST FROM RESEARCH BRIEF OR ESTIMATE FROM THEIR CONTENT]

4. SOCIAL PROOF SECTION
- Testimonials (use placeholders if not available: "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — [Client Name], [Business Name]")
- Numbers if available: [X clients served, X years, X results achieved]
- Any media or award mentions

5. CALL TO ACTION SECTION
- Strong headline: "Ready to [RESULT THEY PROMISE]?"
- Subheadline: "Let's talk. Book your free 30-minute strategy call below."
- CALENDAR EMBED PLACEHOLDER: [Leave a clearly labeled div or button here — we'll add the GHL calendar embed after deploy]

6. FOOTER
- Name / business name
- Simple nav: About, Services, Book a Call
- No clutter

---

DESIGN DIRECTION:
- Tone: [PROFESSIONAL / BOLD / WARM / ENERGETIC — from research brief]
- Color palette: Primary [HEX IF KNOWN, else suggest based on industry], Accent [HEX IF KNOWN]
- Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif. Bold headlines.
- Background: [DARK PREFERRED unless their brand is clearly light/colorful]
- No stock photo feel — keep it clean and real
- Mobile-first

---

CONTENT NOTES:
- Use their real name and business name throughout
- Do NOT use generic filler text ("Lorem ipsum") — write real placeholder copy based on their industry
- The vibe should feel like it was made specifically for them, not a template
- Keep copy tight — every word should earn its place

---

OUTPUT: A single-page landing page, fully responsive, with all sections above. Make it look like a premium boutique agency built it.
```

---

## After Pasting Into Lovable

Common follow-up prompts to refine:

- "Make the hero headline bigger and bolder"
- "Change the CTA button to [color]"
- "Darken the background more"
- "Make the services section use cards instead of a list"
- "Add more white space between sections"
- "The about section is too long — cut it in half"
- "Make it feel more [energetic / professional / warm]"

Keep iterations focused. Ship at 80% — perfect it after the prospect responds.
